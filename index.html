Mental Health & Housing Resources

1

Choose Your Search Method

Option A - Search by Location: You must select at least one option: Enter a zip code, select a state, or choose a service type. You can combine multiple options for more specific results. Note: You cannot search for all services in all states at once.

Option B - Find Near Me: Click "Find Near Me" to use your current location. You can select a search radius (5-50 miles) and optionally filter by service type.

2

View Your Results

Results appear in two views:

  • List View: See all services with details like name, location, phone, and email
  • Map View: See services on an interactive map with a heat map showing service density
3

Using the Map View

The map provides a visual way to explore services:

  • Heat Map (Default): Shows service density with colors - brighter red/yellow areas mean more services in that location. This helps you quickly identify areas with many resources.
  • Individual Markers: Blue pins show each service location. Click on a marker to see the organization name and details. Markers cluster together when zoomed out to prevent clutter.
  • Toggle Views: Use the "Show Heat Map" checkbox to switch between heat map view and individual markers
  • Zoom & Pan: Zoom in to see individual markers, zoom out to see density patterns. Drag the map to explore different areas
  • Fit to Results: Click "üìç Fit to Results" button to automatically zoom the map to show all your search results
  • Map Legend: The legend at the bottom explains what the colors and markers mean
4

üí° Tips

  • You don't need to fill all fields - search by state only, service type only, or any combination
  • If no results appear, try expanding your search (remove filters or increase search radius)

  • Results are sorted by distance when using "Find Near Me"

  • The map automatically updates when you change your search filters
